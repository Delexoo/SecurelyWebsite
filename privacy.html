PRIVACY

At Securely, privacy is not an afterthought—it's our foundation. We believe that security and privacy go hand in hand, and we've designed our application with privacy-first principles from the ground up.

DATA COLLECTION

Securely is designed to protect your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary for the application to function:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or identifiable data.
  • Local Processing: All screen analysis and threat detection happens locally on your device. Your screen content never leaves your computer.
  • Threat Database Updates: We may download threat database updates, but these contain only threat signatures, not personal data.

HOW WE USE DATA

Any data processed by Securely is used solely for real-time threat detection and analysis, improving threat detection accuracy, and application functionality. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

SCREEN MONITORING

Securely monitors your screen to detect threats, but all processing happens locally on your device. Screen content is never stored or transmitted. You can pause or disable monitoring at any time.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have complete control over your data. You can disable screen monitoring at any time, uninstall the application at any time, and request information about what data is processed.
