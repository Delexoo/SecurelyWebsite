WHAT IS SECURELY?

Securely is an AI-powered cybersecurity monitoring application that protects you from phishing attacks, malware, and malicious URLs in real-time. Unlike traditional security tools that only protect files or networks, Securely operates at the visual layer - monitoring everything that appears on your screen before you can interact with it.

Using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology and machine learning, Securely continuously scans your screen, extracts visible text, detects URLs, and analyzes them for threats. When a malicious link or suspicious pattern is detected, you receive an instant alert.
